Looking to get involved on Campus? Do you care deeply about improving representation for all people? You're in luck! Quae is looking for people just like you to join our brand ambassador program!

As a Quae Brand Ambassador you'll become the face of Quae on campus and around Hoboken. You'll attend events with us, spread awareness about Quae, and help us achieve our mission: ensure everyone is represented fairly.

What You'll Do

  • Attend events in Quae gear
  • Post polls and proposals regularly on Quae
  • Add Quae to your social media bios
  • Repost/reshare content
  • Help spread the mission
  • Generate or help generate ideas for social media content
  • Stay up to date on current events

What We're Looking For

  • Outgoing & charismatic
  • Good communication skills
  • Active on social media
  • Believe strongly in the Quae mission
  • ~4 Hours/week

Benefits

  • Free Quae Merch
  • Work alongside the Quae team and gain startup experience
  • Become more involved in the Stevens and Hoboken Communities
